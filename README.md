## About Me 

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Higher Education Administration and Policy program at the University of Florida (Triple Gator 🐊🐊🐊), graduating Spring 2027. My work focuses on how generative AI can be used to model and improve student success, particularly for historically underrepresented students in STEM. 

## Research Focus

I build AI agents that simulate how students learn, interact, and make decisions over time, enabling higher education institutions to test and evaluate what works before implementing real-world interventions.
- Agentic AI and simulation of student learning and decision making
- STEM equity and gateway course success
- AI-informed intervention design
- STEM performance, persistence, and self-efficacy
